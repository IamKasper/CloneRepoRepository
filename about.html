About Human Benchmark Games

What is Human Benchmark?

Human Benchmark Games is a collection of interactive cognitive tests designed to measure various aspects of human performance and mental abilities. Our platform offers scientifically-inspired games that test reaction time, memory, processing speed, and hand-eye coordination.

Our Mission

We believe that understanding your cognitive strengths and weaknesses can help you improve in everyday tasks, gaming, sports, and professional activities. Our games provide instant feedback and allow you to track your progress over time.

Simply select any game from the homepage and follow the on-screen instructions. Each game will provide clear directions before you start. Try to beat your personal best or challenge friends to see who has the best cognitive performance!

Benefits of Cognitive Training

Regular practice with these types of games can help improve:

  • Mental processing speed
  • Working memory capacity
  • Focus and attention span
  • Hand-eye coordination
  • Pattern recognition abilities
